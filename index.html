Core convictions

What I believe

Judgment > Process

AI has commoditised execution. A leader's value isn't their ability to follow a framework — it's their human judgment to know when to break it.

Roadmaps are stories

A roadmap is not a spreadsheet of features. It's a narrative arc of user transformation. If your team can't tell the story, they can't build the product.

Kill the admin tax

Bureaucracy scales faster than impact. I lead by removing the translation layers — those coordination meetings that act as a tax on a team's potential.

Trust is the moat

Anyone can build a model, but few can build a trust layer. In high-stakes domains like mental health, clinical ethics is our greatest competitive advantage.
