Speaker reel

Headshot & bio

Short bio (50 words): Protik Roychowdhury is VP Product at Intellect, Asia's largest mental health platform serving 4M+ users. A three-time founder (one exit) and ICF-certified coach, he brings 15+ years of experience shipping products at scale across Grab, Ola, and Exotel. Published researcher with 3 academic papers.

Long bio (100 words): Protik Roychowdhury is VP Product at Intellect, where he leads product, design, and AI for Asia's largest mental health platform — 4M+ users across APAC, ANZ, China, and Latam, $20M ARR. A three-time founder and IIT Kharagpur alumnus, Protik has spent 15+ years shipping products across marketplace, platform, and e-commerce — now focused on AI. Before Intellect, he led Growth, Engagement & Loyalty at Grab ($3.8B+ revenue superapp) and held product leadership roles at Ola, Exotel, and Fabelio. A published researcher (3 papers on AI in mental health) and ICF-certified career coach. Featured in YourStory, Economic Times, and MobiHealthNews.

Research

Academic publications

Preprint research on AI safety in mental health, therapist quality, and LLM bias — published on JMIR Preprints, medRxiv, and PsyArXiv.

JMIR Preprints · Aug 2025

The RISE Protocol: Reducing Time-to-Intervention in AI-Driven Mental Health Risk Detection

A proposed framework addressing the Safety Paradox — where responsible AI practices can inadvertently delay intervention in time-critical crises. Sole author.
medRxiv · Sep 2025

The Provider GPA: A Composite Framework for Evaluating Digital Therapist Quality

A multidimensional metric combining engagement, reliability, and client feedback to evaluate digital therapist quality at scale. Co-authored with Oliver Sündermann.
PsyArXiv · Sep 2025

Bias in Large Language Models for Mental Health: Vignette-Based Evaluation Across Nine Models

Evidence of systematic biases in LLM mental health assessments across demographics. Co-authored with Lerh, Fang, Sündermann, Sinha & Chauhan.
