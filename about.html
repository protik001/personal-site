My story

I'm VP Product at Intellect, where I lead product, design, content, localisation, and AI across Asia's largest mental health platform — 4M+ users across APAC, ANZ, China, and Latam. I drove enterprise revenue to $20M ARR by pivoting the business from a consumer app to a complete B2B2C care ecosystem.

As a three-time founder, I know firsthand the exhilaration and the grind of building something from nothing. I've taken products from zero to one, scaled teams across geographies, and navigated everything from seed rounds to public-company growth targets.

My passion sits at the intersection of technology and human potential — using AI, personalisation, and behavioural science to create products that measurably improve lives.

Outside of product, I'm an ICF-certified career coach. I work with PMs, founders, and leaders who want a structured, empathetic partner to help them navigate career transitions, scale their impact, and lead with clarity.
