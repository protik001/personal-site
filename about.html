Background

My story

I'm VP Product at Intellect, where I lead product, design, content, localisation, and AI for a regulated, multi-tenant enterprise health platform serving 4M+ users across APAC, ANZ, China, and Latam. I drove enterprise revenue to $20M ARR by pivoting the business from a consumer app to a complete B2B2C care ecosystem — configurable compliance, multi-market deployment, and cross-border infrastructure.

My career spans enterprise B2B SaaS, two-sided marketplaces, high-stakes regulated technology, and consumer super-apps. As a three-time founder, I know firsthand the exhilaration and the grind of building something from nothing. I've taken products from zero to one, scaled teams across geographies, and navigated everything from seed rounds to public-company growth targets.

Most of my work involves figuring out how to make ML and personalisation actually useful at scale — matching people to the right care, pricing rides correctly, detecting fraud before it costs millions.

Outside of product, I'm an ICF-certified career coach. I work with PMs, founders, and leaders who want a structured, empathetic partner to help them navigate career transitions, scale their impact, and lead with clarity.

Philosophy

How I think about product

My career has been defined by building products where getting it wrong has real consequences — from marketplace fraud at Ola to clinical safety at Intellect. The through-line is trust: how to build it at scale, how to protect it under pressure, and how to make it a competitive advantage rather than a compliance checkbox.

I believe the best product leaders are defined not by the frameworks they follow but by the judgment they exercise when those frameworks break down. AI has made this more true, not less. The PMs who thrive in 2026 won't be the ones with the tightest processes — they'll be the ones who know when to break them.

As a coach, I bring the same philosophy. I work with PMs, founders, and leaders who want a structured, empathetic partner to help them navigate career transitions and lead with more clarity. I believe everyone has the answers within them — they just need the right questions, frameworks, and space to find them.
